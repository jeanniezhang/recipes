how to get out of bed in five simple steps

the human condition is marked by melancholia, and insurmountable hardships, yet in so many i see unwavering determination. sry not you tho - your mum, circa 2003

ingredients

  • a (preferably human) (preferably yours) (preferable sleeping) (preferable not unalive) body
  • bed
  • legs, or other appendage
  • a life decision you feel a strong sense of regret over

steps

  1. (involuntarily) open your eyes
  2. close your eyes
  3. search for a reason to get up
  4. keep searching
  5. i hope you find it
